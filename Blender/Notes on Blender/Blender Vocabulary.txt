Blender Vocabulary:

- 3D ViewPort
~ area that shows the 3D scene.

- Object Origin 
~ represents the object's position,
~ & where transformations occur from.

- Transform Pivot Point
~ point in 3D space where 
~ transformations occur from.

- Orientation (local, global, normal, ect.)
~ refers to a relative direction in 3D space.
